Privacy Policy

Roma Mart Corp., Roma Mart Convenience values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (when submitted via contact forms or direct communication).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, visit timestamps, and pages visited (collected automatically for analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries or feedback submitted through our website or contact forms.
  • To improve website functionality and understand visitor interactions via analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights and business operations.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We may use basic cookies or analytics services (like Google Analytics) to better understand user behavior. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties unless legally required or to operate services you request (e.g., delivery or payment systems).

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, though no digital system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at contact@romamart.ca.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Please revisit this page for any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at contact@romamart.ca.
